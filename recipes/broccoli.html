Pasta with broccoli

Description

Pasta with fresh broccoli seasoned with olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Fresh broccoli
  • Fusilli
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut florets and parts of broccoli stems into bite-size pieces.
  2. Saute broccoli with oil, butter and salt over medium heat for about 12 minutes or until just tender, stirring frequently.
  3. Place hot pasta in a serving dish; top with broccoli mixture.
